About Umbra Security System

Umbra is a home monitoring system which detects gas level, motion waves and light intensity, measures current temperature and listen to home frequency through a microphone.In shadow theory, the umbra is the central cone of darkness. Umbra Security System works in the shadows to ensure your home is safe.

It easily connects to the bluetooth of users phone. The user receives push notifications to their mobile device based on alerts.

Umbra will help you:

  • Detect room temperature and monitor drastic chnages
  • Detect if the lights in your house are turned on or of
  • Detect smoke
  • Detect alarm going off to give you notification if something is wrong
  • Detect motion and alert user of unusual behaviour within the house
  • Digital clock
